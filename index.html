Safe Transport Victoria x Grand Prix Campaign

A campaign microsite, Safe Transport Victoria aimed to bring safety and awareness surrounding taxi and ridesharing at Melbourne's F1 Grand Prix.

With a tight 7-day deadline, an agile approach was adopted amongst designers and developers and Safe Transport Victoria's design system was used. The final outcome is a user-friendly, accessible interface, with a clean design that aligns with the brand's identity and campaign.
